Featured Project

Off-Road All-Terrain Vehicle | Roll Cage Design & Structural Validation Support

Single-Seat Off-Road ATV Platform Development

Role: SolidWorks Designer Focus: Roll Cage + Assemblies Tools: SolidWorks, Weldments, ANSYS (with CAE team)

I contributed to the end-to-end design of a single-seat off-road all-terrain vehicle, with primary ownership of the roll-cage architecture and integration support for major assemblies. Using SolidWorks Weldments, part modeling, and assembly-level workflows, I developed a manufacturable tubular frame, validated packaging clearances, and supported full-vehicle digital build-up.

In collaboration with simulation engineers, I assisted with static structural evaluation of the roll cage under front, rear, and side impact load cases to assess occupant protection behavior under demanding loading conditions. Deformation and equivalent stress contour outputs were used to identify reinforcement opportunities and improve structural robustness.

My Contributions

  • Developed roll-cage geometry using SolidWorks weldment members.
  • Built and maintained part/assembly structure for full-vehicle integration.
  • Supported suspension/chassis packaging and clearance checks.
  • Prepared design iterations based on CAE feedback.

Validation Scope

  • Front impact case: deformation + von Mises stress review.
  • Rear impact case: deformation + stress distribution review.
  • Side impact case: deformation + stress hotspot identification.
  • Safety-driven iteration for improved cage stiffness/energy path.
